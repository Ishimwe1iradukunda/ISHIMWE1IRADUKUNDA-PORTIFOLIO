
## Professional Full Stack Developer Portfolio

A dark, minimal, and visually stunning portfolio with rich animations and a full showcase of your work and experience. Here's what we'll build:

---

### 🎨 Design System
- **Dark theme** with deep blacks, dark grays, and a vibrant accent color (electric blue or cyan gradient)
- **Smooth rich animations**: scroll-reveal effects, typing animation in hero, hover micro-interactions, parallax-style movements
- **Fully responsive** — pixel-perfect on mobile, tablet, and desktop

---

### 📄 Pages & Sections

1. **Hero Section** — Full-screen opening with animated typing effect cycling through roles ("Full Stack Developer", "Cloud Engineer", "Problem Solver"), a glowing CTA button, and floating tech icons in the background

2. **About Section** — A personal intro with a profile picture placeholder, short bio, and a few highlight stats (years of experience, projects built, etc.)

3. **Skills Section** — Categorized skill cards with animated progress bars or icon grids covering:
   - Frontend Frameworks (React, Vue, etc.)
   - Backend Languages (Node.js, Python, etc.)
   - DevOps & Cloud (AWS, Docker, CI/CD, etc.)
   - Databases (PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis, etc.)
   - Other tools

4. **Projects Section** — Filterable project cards with hover effects showing:
   - Project title, description, tech stack badges
   - Live demo & GitHub links
   - Placeholder cards ready for your real projects

5. **Experience Section** — A vertical animated timeline of work history with company, role, dates, and key achievements

6. **Testimonials Section** — Rotating/carousel testimonial cards from colleagues or clients (placeholder content)

7. **Blog Section** — A preview grid of recent articles with title, date, and tags (placeholders, link-ready for a blog platform)

8. **Contact Section** — A working contact form with:
   - Name, Email, Subject, Message fields
   - Form validation with Zod
   - Submit functionality (will use EmailJS or a mailto fallback)
   - Social links (GitHub, LinkedIn, Twitter)

---

### ✨ Animations & UX
- **Intersection Observer** scroll-reveal for every section entering the viewport
- **Typing effect** in the hero headline
- **Staggered card animations** when sections come into view
- **Smooth scrolling** navigation with active link highlighting
- **Sticky navbar** that changes style on scroll

---

### 🧭 Navigation
- Fixed top navbar with logo/name, section links, and a "Hire Me" CTA button
- Mobile hamburger menu
- Active section tracking as user scrolls

All content will use **placeholder data** (your name, projects, experience) that you can easily swap with your real details using Visual Edits or by sharing them with me.
